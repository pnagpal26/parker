# Parker Instagram Story Ads

## What This Is

5 Instagram story ad creatives (9:16 format) for Parker at Yonge + Eglinton, Toronto.
Each ad targets a different funnel stage with a distinct visual theme and CTA.
Generated using nano-banana (Google Gemini CLI) with Parker brand assets and color system.

## Core Value

Prospects see Parker as a premium, move-in-ready option and take immediate action — whether that's booking a tour, chatting with Emma, or claiming the free-month incentive.
